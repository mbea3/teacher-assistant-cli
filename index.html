Project Owner Spotlight

Inspired by artists shaping culture, storytelling, and creativity.

Innovation is not a straight line. It evolves.

From DJ turntables to artificial intelligence, Monique Beacham has always explored how technology shapes human experience. Through the TourDeBuzz project, she reimagined live music as more than sound. It became connection, culture, and immersive storytelling powered by creative tech.

But it does not stop there.

With over fourteen years at Apple and advanced studies at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Monique continues to push innovation forward at the intersection of AI, sustainability, and community impact. As the founder of Quanimm AI, she is building the next chapter of responsible technology.

From shaping live music experiences to advancing sustainable artificial intelligence, the mission remains the same. Create technology that moves culture forward while respecting the world it operates in.

  • Leadership Approach: Collaborative and impact-focused
